Hi there!

Welcome to SpaceX Monitor, one of my little side-projects.
It provides, or at least attempts to provide, a clean and easy to use web-interface for the data provided by SpaceX API. Among the data is a list of all launches and launch vehicles the company SpaceX sends screaming through the atmosphere on daring adventures.

I started this project to try out a few new or new-to-me web technologies. For starters this is a Progressive Web App (PWA). This means that upon loading, this app will cache itself using a smart little script called a 'service-worker'.
A PWA app can, in some cases, even be installed and used offline on a smartphone regardless its brand or operating system.
Holy cow, isn't that awesome?!
Besides that, it also gave me a good reason to set-up HTTPS on my domain. Since a PWA always exclusively utilizes the HTTPS protocol. Over time I have changed a lot about this app. Recently I have given the look and feel a big overhaul and, while I was at it, changed the underlying structure governing data streams using ReactiveX for JavaScript.
I hope you like it!
